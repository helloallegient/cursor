Raseel gujral ansal

Raseel Gujral Ansal, is a celebrated and innovative force in the world of multi-disciplinary design for close to four decades and brings her unparalleled expertise and artistic vision to each exploration. Known for her pioneering work in interior, spatial, and product design, Raseel is renowned for her ability to blend art, design, and heritage seamlessly.

The Mission

At Raseel Gujral Ansal, our mission is to shape a dialogue between heritage and modernity—through spaces, objects, and experiences that celebrate Indian soul with global sophistication. We strive to reframe artisanal legacy as contemporary luxury, honouring craftsmanship, storytelling, and cultural provenance in every creation. Guided by intuition, artistry, and innovation, we design with purpose to evoke emotion, elevate everyday beauty, and inspire a more connected world of design.

The Vision

To be a timeless force in design that reclaims and reimagines India’s artistic legacy for a global stage. We envision a world where craftsmanship is revered, spaces speak of soul, and design becomes a vessel for cultural continuity, creative freedom, and emotional resonance. Through our work, we aspire to nurture living traditions rooted in memory, layered with meaning and ever evolving.

Raseel Gujral Ansal embodies a refined balance between art, architecture, and artisanal luxury. Her brand is a mirror of duality rooted in heritage and expressed through contemporary design. Each creation is a journey through cultural memory, sacred geometry, and modern form, expressing a distinctive dialogue between inner essence and outward experience.

The design ethos is rooted in paradox where structure meets spontaneity, and traditional symbolism merges with cutting-edge modernism. Her practice is immersive and intuitive, blending storytelling, spatial rhythm, and tactile richness. She views design not merely as a product, but as an evolving narrative shaped by material, light, and memory.
