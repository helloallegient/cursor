A creative continuum unbound by time, alive in every frame. The RGA design philosophy is conceived as an evolving process unbound by the rigidity of linear timelines. There are no trends here, only timeless conversations. An immersive expression of the many facets that define the creative continuum of Raseel Gujral Ansal. Rather than presenting a singular narrative, the approach offers a relaxed and intuitive navigation across decades of work and disciplines; past, present, and future. Each project stands on its own, yet speaks to a greater whole. The practice resists the conventional format of a themed vocabulary; instead, it embraces what might be called a non-conventional repository of a spontaneous gathering of memories. The multi-disciplinary design practice is a crucible for incubation of spatial design, bespoke furniture, art, collectibles, and home accents.
